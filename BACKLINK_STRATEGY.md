### 1.2 Link Building Tactics

#### Tactic 1: HARO (Help A Reporter Out)
- Respond to journalist queries about AI, automation, SMB technology
- Position Katherine/founders as expert sources
- Target: 2-3 media mentions per quarter

#### Tactic 2: Guest Posting
- Pitch original research on SMB automation adoption
- Contribute "state of automation" reports
- Share client case studies (with permission)

#### Tactic 3: Digital PR
- Publish quarterly "Automation Impact Reports"
- Create shareable infographics on automation ROI
- Announce client wins and partnerships

#### Tactic 4: Broken Link Building
- Find broken links on relevant resource pages
- Offer service pages as replacements
- Focus on automation, SMB, and technology resources

#### Tactic 5: Competitor Backlink Analysis
- Analyze backlinks to competitors
- Identify replicable link opportunities
- Target sites that link to multiple competitors
